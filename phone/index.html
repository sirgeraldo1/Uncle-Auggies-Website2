THE VOID

Uncle Auggie wanted a natural hair product without shelling out major cash. He decided to simply make it.

DESIGN

Months later, he created the notoriously affordable and effective pomade. He found additional uses too, especially on an irritated beard & dry skin.

TESTING

After using daily for months, he gave samples to friends and adjusted the recipe for the most desired result: invisible style.

FULL RELEASE

After thorough research, Uncle Auggie scoffed at the inflated profits of competing products.  The fair price allows for everyone to enjoy the fruits of his labor.

ABOUT AUGGIE

The original Uncle Auggie, August Sr., was born in the 1854. His namesake soon became a family treasure and was eventually passed down to his Great Great Nephew.
